About

qtradio is an online radio station created by me, ohlookitsderpy, so I could listen to my weeb tunes wherever and whenever. When starting this I already knew about listen.moe, but didn't like the idea of having to pay to queue your own songs :^). Usually I add music whenever I find some good tunes. If you have suggestions, join and ping me in my Discord!

FAQ

Q: How many songs are there?

A: Over 100, with more being added all the time!

Q: Is this project opensource?

A: Portions of it are, check my GitHub for the parts which are.

Q: Can I suggest songs?

A: Not at the moment, though I may accept suggestions soon.
